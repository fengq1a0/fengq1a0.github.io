I'm a CIS Ph.D. student at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. Lingjie Liu. My research is 3D Vision, the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics, with a current focus on 3D human reconstruction. I'm exploring more general 3D understanding, reconstruction, and generation.

Before that, I received my Master's and Bachelor's degrees from Tianjin University, advised by Prof. Kun Li, working closely with Prof. Yebin Liu and Prof. Yu-Kun Lai.
